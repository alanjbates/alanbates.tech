About me


I love building end to end data solutions that enable organizations to maximize the value of data. By leveraging a broad skill set and 15 years of experience, I am able to bring data products from idea into reality. I also hold two degrees, a Masters of Science in Data Science along with a Bachelors of Science in Business Administration.

Data driven solutions can drive organizations forward and help you reach your goals more efficiently. They can also disrupt traditional ways of operating and create competitive advantage. To successfully build these solutions I have learned the skills required to deliver while operating in many different roles.

This end to end approach to data solutions is why I consider myself a Data Practitioner. I believe that a data practitioner should be able to flex into any role that is required to deliver the data product. From source system business analysis, technical systems analysis, data architecture and design, ETL/ELT, big data pipelines, modern data warehousing, and predictive business intelligence, all the way to productionalized real time data science products, we can make the world a better place, one SELECT * at a time :)
